Corporate Capability Statement

Solomon Kin LLC is a certified minority-owned prime contractor specializing in advanced high-assurance identity verification, digital forensics, enterprise records governance, and cross-industry contract fulfillment tracking loops.

Core Competencies

We deliver integrated technological architectures and operational human capital personnel to satisfy multi-industry procurement demands. Our strengths map directly across four high-demand segments:

  • High-Assurance Digital Identity (IDV): Biometric enrollment protocols, hardware-bound cryptographic credentials, and decentralized token loops engineered via our REALAiID.com foundations.
  • Enterprise Records Management: Secure data-vaulting models, permanent tracking of high-security employee rosters, compliance tracking, and automated document lifecycle archiving.
  • Social Services & Human Capital Personnel: Scalable deployment of case managers, counselors, university bursar/financial specialists, and compliance operators equipped with secure data layers.
  • Turnkey Infrastructure Logistics: End-to-end multi-tier contract oversight, extensive heavy machinery fleet rental models, and rapid onboarding metrics for specialized subcontractor units.
